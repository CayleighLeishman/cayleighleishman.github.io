Welcome to New Threads Quilting! A small business where you bring in your newly designed quilt tops and bottoms and Darci adds thread patterns to make your awesome quilt look even more professionally done! Darci is dedicated to giving your unique quilts the best quality she has to offer to ensure the best for your quilts. She understands the value of a homemade quilt and puts in only the best into her work. She has assistants running in the background to help assist where they can to ensure you get a swift response.

In order to help Darci help you, finish your top piece of your quilt, the bottom piece of your quilt and bring it in to Darci! You will have the opportunity to pick out from different pattern designs like different flowers, zebra stripes, ocean waves or many more! To see some of those threaded designs go to “Patterns” in the navigation link.
